About

Hello, welcome to the train booking website. This website was created to enable the students using the train to go to School to be able to purchase the train ticket on time.

The train's destination is only one which is the Moringa School. The train passes through three places so that it could arrive at its destination. The train's departure from the first station is usually at 6:00 am in the morning.

The train arrives at the second pick-up destination between 7:00 and 7:30, and then it arrives at the third pick-up destination between 8:00 and 8:30. The train will arrive at the final destination, which is the school, at exactly 9:00 am.

For the price, it is usually very friendly and it doesn't fluctuate because this is a charity to the community website.
